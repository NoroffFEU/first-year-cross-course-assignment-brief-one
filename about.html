About rainydays

Rainy days is a norwegian company selling jackets for men and women whose interests are; being outdoors, hiking, exploring, skiing, camping and canoeing.

We offer a range of quality jackets at a manageable price. Our jackets are high quality that emphasize durability and being suitable for a range of different weather types. Our goal is to ensure that our customers are comfortable whatever adventure they go on.
